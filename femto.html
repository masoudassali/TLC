How TLC works:

The Lined Canvas (TLC) is an AI-powered tool that converts your uploaded images into high-quality, stylized line art outlines.

  1. Upload your image (JPEG, PNG, or TIFF, max 7MB).
  2. Optionally specify a celebrity to help the AI focus.
  3. Customize the style using the presets (Influential Artists or Art Style) and the Page Busyness gauge.
  4. Hit 'Cook' and Chef Tony will generate a unique, printable outline for you.

This outline is perfect for personal coloring, tracing, or as a base for your own artwork.

Image types allowed: JPEG/JPG, PNG, TIFF. Image size limit: **7mbs.**
